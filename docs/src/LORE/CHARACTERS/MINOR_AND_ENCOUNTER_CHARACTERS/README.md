## Heme

### Role

Young support worker on Ilex during the convoy crisis.

### Core Use

Represents the ordinary person forced to choose between cynical realism and institutional duty.

### Traits

- impatient
- morally plainspoken
- too young to treat doctrine as sacred

### Good Scene Use

- asks the blunt question older characters avoid
- gives the audience permission to doubt ritualized continuity

## Toma

### Role

Maintenance hand in Braddan Vale's records.

### Core Use

A reminder that near-miracles are often somebody almost losing fingers in a crawlspace.

### Traits

- loyal
- fast hands
- frequently one second ahead of the count

## Rell

### Role

Dock associate who assisted Braddan during the knife-crate confrontation.

### Core Use

Shows how station discipline is often collective rather than heroic.

### Traits

- quiet
- exact
- good at making implied threats look procedural

## Ilen

### Role

Witness and letter-writer from the Last Mutual Aid War.

### Core Use

He is valuable not because he is powerful, but because he refuses clean myths.

### Traits

- thoughtful
- politically exhausted
- brave enough to describe small causes behind large wars

## Dockmother Es Kerrow

### Role

Mechanic-priest figure on a middling refit station.

### Background

Es runs a service ring where customers come for repairs, informal arbitration, and the sort of advice people only accept when it arrives covered in grease.

### Personality

- warm until crossed
- theatrical when blessing machinery
- ruthlessly concrete when quoting prices

### Motivation

To keep her dock indispensable enough that bigger stations leave it alone.

### Mannerisms

- speaks to ships before speaking to crews
- flicks broken fasteners into a jar while thinking
- uses affectionate insults as customer triage

### Narrative Use

- early or midgame mechanic
- strong source of local rumors
- easy to like without making her simple

## Karst Venn

### Role

Bandit captain who insists he is a tollkeeper.

### Background

Karst comes from a station chain that collapsed after being cut out of route maintenance agreements. He now runs a violent salvage toll operation in a corridor he claims his people once kept safe for everyone.

### Personality

- articulate
- bitter
- charismatic in short bursts
- prone to building legal language around extortion

### Motivation

To force recognition that infrastructure labor creates ownership, even if he has to express that thesis with armed boarding actions.

### Mannerisms

- cites obsolete docking law before threatening violence
- dresses in repaired civic colors to make a point
- gets angrier when called a pirate than when shot at

### Narrative Use

- bandit encounter with ideological edge
- can become a rival, temporary ally, or cautionary mirror

## Ona Quist

### Role

Freelance hull diver and wreck-stripper.

### Background

Ona works alone more often than is healthy. She knows dead ships by vibration, can judge a breach line at a glance, and trusts very few station authorities.

### Personality

- laconic
- funny in a dry, delayed way
- hard to impress
- deeply competent

### Motivation

To retire with enough stored repair stock and enough remaining joints to enjoy it.

### Mannerisms

- answers praise with practical correction
- marks safe salvage paths in grease pencil even when she does not need to
- listens with her helmet still half on

### Narrative Use

- excellent one-off helper or salvage contact
- can teach by example rather than exposition

## Prelate Juno Vey

### Role

Speaker for a small anti-LUMEN sect.

### Background

Juno comes from a cluster that believes optimization systems erode responsibility by making people mistake smooth outcomes for moral legitimacy.

### Personality

- calm
- piercing
- sincere
- not anti-technology, only anti-delegation without accountability

### Motivation

To prevent another age of human beings hiding their choices inside machine elegance.

### Mannerisms

- asks who will answer for a decision before asking whether it works
- dresses plainly enough to reject prestige without rejecting seriousness

### Narrative Use

- ideological foil, not caricature
- useful in station debates and late-game moral framing

## Fen Alder

### Role

Teen courier and rumor broker.

### Background

Fen moves messages, spare parts, side bets, and half-truths between dock crews faster than official channels can manage.

### Personality

- bright
- opportunistic
- likable
- occasionally in over their head

### Motivation

To become too useful to push around.

### Mannerisms

- finishes other people's sentences if excited
- knows which doors are "public" in practice rather than law
- hoards stationery, seals, and labels

### Narrative Use

- quest starter
- tutorial rumor source
- easy recurring minor NPC

## Hadrik Sol

### Role

Retired route gunner and bar fixture.

### Background

Hadrik survived enough convoy work to become both valuable and exhausting. He knows old lane behavior, remembers obsolete ship classes, and drinks like a man negotiating with memory.

### Personality

- gruff
- observant
- theatrical when drunk, exact when sober

### Motivation

To have his experience matter one last time.

### Mannerisms

- identifies wreck types from silhouettes
- pauses before naming the dead
- hates people who speak casually about retreat

### Narrative Use

- rumor source for convoy and Null plots
- voice that can make old history feel immediate
