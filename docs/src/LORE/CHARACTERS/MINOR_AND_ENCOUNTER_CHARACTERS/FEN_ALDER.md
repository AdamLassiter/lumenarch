# Fen Alder

## Profile

Teen courier and rumor broker.

Fen moves messages, spare parts, side bets, and half-truths between dock crews faster than official channels can manage.

They are bright, opportunistic, likable, and occasionally in over their head. They want to become too useful to push around.

They finish other people's sentences if excited, know which doors are "public" in practice rather than law, and hoard stationery, seals, and labels.

## Backstory Fragment: Inventory of Pockets

Two red labels, unused. One blue label, already used but not in a way anyone can prove. Three message slips. Half a valve charm from Pio because he thinks I do not know it is a training token. One screw from Dockmother Es's jar, which may be cursed but was definitely unattended.

Nera Vale says a station survives by knowing what moves through it.

I move through it.

Therefore I am civic infrastructure.

Do not write that where Nera can see it.

Today's rumor prices: one sweet for patrol gossip, two sweets for route gossip, actual protein for anything involving Aven Rusk. Hadrik says Null escorts used to blink before firing. Hadrik also says he never snores, so weight accordingly.

Future plan: become indispensable, then respectable, then rich enough to own a drawer no one searches.
