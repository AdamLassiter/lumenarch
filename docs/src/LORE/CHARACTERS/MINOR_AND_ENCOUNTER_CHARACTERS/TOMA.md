# Toma

## Profile

Maintenance hand in Braddan Vale's records.

Toma is a reminder that near-miracles are often somebody almost losing fingers in a crawlspace. He is loyal, fast-handed, and frequently one second ahead of the count.

## Backstory Fragment: Tool Crib Log

Toma returned three spanners, one lamp, no gloves.

Asked about the gloves, he showed both hands, all fingers present, and said this was "a better inventory result than seemed likely at the time."

Braddan Vale's note in the margin: charge him for the gloves after praising him privately.

The crawlspace record says Toma reached the manual latch before the pressure differential tore the crate stack loose. It does not say he laughed afterward until he vomited. It does not say he kissed the cracked valve casing. Official records have poor manners around relief.

Toma kept working for nine more hours with his hands wrapped in insulation cloth. When someone called him brave, he said brave would have meant reading the stencil before crawling under a falling knife-crate.

Later saint-makers painted him kneeling beside Braddan in clean blue light.

He would have preferred better gloves.
