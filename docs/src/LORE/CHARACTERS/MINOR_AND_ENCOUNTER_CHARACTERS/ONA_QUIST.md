# Ona Quist

## Profile

Freelance hull diver and wreck-stripper.

Ona works alone more often than is healthy. She knows dead ships by vibration, can judge a breach line at a glance, and trusts very few station authorities.

She is laconic, funny in a dry, delayed way, hard to impress, and deeply competent. She wants to retire with enough stored repair stock and enough remaining joints to enjoy it.

She answers praise with practical correction, marks safe salvage paths in grease pencil even when she does not need to, and listens with her helmet still half on.

## Backstory Fragment: Helmet Recorder

Wreck is singing through the starboard ribs. Low note. Bad note.

Client says sensor map shows clear passage. Client can marry sensor map if it keeps them warm.

Marking safe path anyway. Yellow grease. Three turns, duck under the split cable, do not touch the pretty frost because pretty frost means your suit has opinions for the last time.

Found galley still sealed. Four cups mag-locked to the table. One cup says BEST ROUTE DAD. Taking the cups. Someone will want them or someone will need to learn wanting.

Knee hurts. Left wrist worse. Retire after three more good strips, I said twelve strips ago.

Note to self: buy better socks. Death is uncertain. Damp feet are not.
