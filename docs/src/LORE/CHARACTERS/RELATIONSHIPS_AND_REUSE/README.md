## Character Reuse Suggestions

### Good recurring contacts

- Nera Vale
- Aven Rusk
- Seli Dorn
- Yara Sen
- Tal Iri

These characters each open a different thematic door and can recur without feeling repetitive.

### Good one-scene scene-stealers

- Jessa
- Ona Quist
- Fen Alder
- Hadrik Sol
- Karst Venn

These are the sorts of characters who can define a place or conflict quickly.

### Good absent presences

- Sera Tann
- Braddan Vale
- Mira
- the Blue-Veil Quartermaster
- the Preserved Subject

These figures are strongest when discovered through traces.

## Useful Tensions for Future Writing

- Nera Vale would likely respect Seli Dorn while disagreeing with her scale politics.
- Aven Rusk and Karst Venn are cousins in worldview separated by polish and self-image.
- Tal Iri and Prelate Juno Vey could argue fruitfully for hours without either becoming foolish.
- Yara Sen and Hadrik Sol would agree on practical caution while differing on how much myth to permit around machine anomalies.
- Fen Alder is exactly the sort of person Aven Rusk would either mentor or exploit.
