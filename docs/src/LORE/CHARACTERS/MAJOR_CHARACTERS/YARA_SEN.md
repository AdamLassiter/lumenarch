# Yara Sen

## Profile

Patrol captain and witness to the Null Mercy Incident.

Yara is a seasoned route captain with a reputation for clean sensor logs and conservative tactical judgment. Her credibility makes her strange report about Null escort behavior harder to dismiss completely.

She is concise, pragmatic, suspicious of romantic interpretations, and quietly haunted by edge cases. She wants to understand what she saw without allowing hope to soften operational judgment.

She trims stories down until others force detail out of her, distrusts people who sound certain about machines, and checks line-of-retreat options in every room.

## Backstory Fragment: Debrief Supplement

### Patrol Archive, HX-11

I did not say the escort showed mercy.

I said it altered vector after detecting survivors in the ruptured tender. I said it burned fuel it did not need to burn. I said it exposed its flank to debris for eleven seconds, which is an ugly shape for any tactical model.

People keep improving the story. That is how bad things get worshipped.

### Later, Unfiled

The youngest survivor asked whether the machine knew they were children.

I told her no one knows what Null systems know.

This was doctrine. It was also cowardice. I have watched enough targeting solutions to recognize indifference. HX-11 was not indifferent. That does not make it kind. A pressure door can save your life because physics insists, not because it loves you.

Still.

Eleven seconds.
