## Backstory Fragment: Reply Never Sent

### Draft, Recovered from a Heat-Damaged Slate

Ilen,

You always write like confession is a repair procedure. Identify fault, name pressure, replace damaged part, seal.

War does not seal.

You ask me to preserve the true causes. Fine. I will write that the pact failed because the eastern tanks were half full, because three councilors wanted election banners more than shared valves, because frightened people prefer a clean villain to a bad spreadsheet. I will write that your side lied less and still lied.

I will also write that you saved nineteen children during the lock riot and then let men use those children as proof that their fathers were righteous.

Do not ask me to forgive the record. I can only keep it from becoming useful too quickly.

If this reaches you, burn my old blue coat. It smells of smoke and meetings.

Mira
