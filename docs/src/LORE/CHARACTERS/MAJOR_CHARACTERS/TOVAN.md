# Tovan

## Profile

Expedition specialist whose curiosity tips into surrender before alien coherence.

Tovan is a salvage professional with enough technical confidence to be genuinely dangerous in LUMEN-adjacent contexts. Tovan represents the kind of person who mistakes elegant response for benign intent.

He is exuberant, technically gifted, impatient with caution, and attracted to the feeling of being chosen by a system. He wants to be first through the door that makes everyone else hesitate.

He grins at machines when they respond, narrates risk like a challenge instead of a warning, and moves closer when others stop.

## Backstory Fragment: Suit Recorder, Partial

### Audio Before Descent

Listen to that return tone. No, really listen. It is not echo. Echo decays. This is adjustment.

Tal is making the face they make when they want three more words for humility. Jessa is making the face she makes when a room has teeth. I love them both. I am going in anyway.

### Lower Chamber

Light just braided around my glove. Not reflected. Around.

I know, I know. "Do not anthropomorphize responsive systems." I can hear the training module scolding me in that dead academy voice. But if something reaches back with such care, what do you call the space between warning and welcome?

### Last Clear Packet

It knows the shape of my name.

That is impossible unless names are not what we think they are.

Tell Jessa she was right. Tell Tal not to translate me too neatly.
