# Braddan Vale

## Profile

Historical quartermaster later mythologized as the Salvager Saint.

Braddan is a perfect example of how competence becomes sanctity in Continuant memory. The true shape is hard, fair, unsentimental, mathematically moral, and skilled at turning order into survival.

She is best used through paired records, contradictory memory, and ritual observance grounded in practical crisis.

## Backstory Fragment: Two Versions of the Same Day

### Festival Recitation

Braddan Vale stood beneath the failing lamps and divided the last heat by justice. Her hand did not tremble. Her voice did not break. The knife-crates opened. The hungry were fed. The station lived.

Children throw blue paper seals into the air here.

### Quartermaster Ledger

Crate seven seized after false manifest. Rell performed lockout correctly. Toma injured, not fatally. Three families denied request for additional heat. One appealed using infant illness. Appeal rejected. Infant survived. Grandfather did not.

Braddan's handwriting grows smaller at the bottom of the page.

### Later Annotation

Saints are easier to thank than quartermasters.

Quartermasters leave arithmetic. Arithmetic asks who froze so another room could thaw. The rite keeps Braddan kind because the ledger could not.
